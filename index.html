What is Project Graduation?

Project Graduation is the culmination of a year-long celebration for WHRHS graduates at the end of the school year. It started with the PG committee purchasing and handing out the Class of 2020 T-shirts for the Panoramic Senior Picture Day and continues with numerous social events including Spirit Days, Senior Breakfast and the ever popular Yearbook Signing Ice Cream Social event!

This exciting year ends with a once in a lifetime, alcohol and drug-free event that brings our kids together, safely, for one last time on the night of graduation!

The Plan

Event Date: June 19, 2020

Estimated Time: Bus departs WHRHS approximately 9:15 PM on June 19th and the time at Dave and Busters is from 10:00 pm until 2:30 AM, returning to WHRHS the following morning, June 20th at 3:00 AM.
