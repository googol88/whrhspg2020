What's Project Graduation?

[OLD] Project Graduation is more than just a big party for our graduates at the end of the school year. So many exciting events lead up to Project Graduation. It's a year-long celebration of school spirit! The fun starts off with our PG committee purchasing and handing out the Class of 2018 T-shirts for Panoramic Senior Picture Day and continues throughout the year with Spirit Days, Senior Breakfast and the ever popular Yearbook Signing Ice Cream Social event and ending with a once in a lifetime, alcohol and drug-free event that brings our kids together, safely, for one last time on the night of graduation!

[NEW] Project Graduation is the culmination of a year-long celebration for WHRHS graduates at the end of the school year. It started with the PG committee purchasing and handing out the Class of 2020 T-shirts for the Panoramic Senior Picture Day and continues with numerous social events including Spirit Days, Senior Breakfast and the ever popular Yearbook Signing Ice Cream Social event!

This exciting year ends with a once in a lifetime, alcohol and drug-free event that brings our kids together, safely, for one last time on the night of graduation!
